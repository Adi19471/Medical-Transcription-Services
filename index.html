A Brief History of Medical Transcription's Origin

The history of medical transcription began at the turn of the 20th century. Doctors depended on stenographers at the time because they didn't want to keep their own notes and were able to pretty much shorthand anything. The appearance of typewriters at the turn of the 20th century resulted in an explosion in technological development.
